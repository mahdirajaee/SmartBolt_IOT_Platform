How Smart IoT Bolt Works

Our system provides end-to-end monitoring and analytics for your industrial pipelines.

1

Sensor Installation

Smart IoT Bolts with integrated sensors are installed at strategic points along the pipeline.

2

Data Collection

The sensors continuously collect temperature and pressure data in real-time.

3

Analysis & Prediction

Our analytics engine processes the data, identifies patterns, and predicts potential issues.

4

Alert & Action

Receive alerts and take preventive actions before failures occur, reducing downtime and costs.
