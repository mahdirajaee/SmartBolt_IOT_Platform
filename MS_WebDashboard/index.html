Powerful Pipeline Monitoring Features

Our Smart IoT Bolt system provides comprehensive monitoring capabilities to ensure optimal pipeline performance and safety.

Real-time Monitoring

Monitor pipeline pressure and temperature in real-time with high-precision sensors embedded in our Smart IoT Bolts.

Predictive Analytics

Advanced analytics with ARIMA models to predict potential failures before they occur, allowing for proactive maintenance.

Historical Data Analysis

Access and analyze historical data to identify trends, optimize operations, and improve maintenance strategies.

Intuitive Dashboard

Easy-to-use dashboard with visualizations, alerts, and actionable insights for efficient pipeline management.

Cascade Problem Detection

Advanced algorithms to detect cascade problems along the pipeline, identifying root causes rather than just symptoms.

Alerts & Notifications

Receive instant alerts via telegram and email when anomalies are detected, allowing for quick response and intervention.

How Smart IoT Bolt Works

Our system provides end-to-end monitoring and analytics for your industrial pipelines.

1

Sensor Installation

Smart IoT Bolts with integrated sensors are installed at strategic points along the pipeline.

2

Data Collection

The sensors continuously collect temperature and pressure data in real-time.

3

Analysis & Prediction

Our analytics engine processes the data, identifies patterns, and predicts potential issues.

4

Alert & Action

Receive alerts and take preventive actions before failures occur, reducing downtime and costs.
